The Free Software community is composed of a large number of wnderful people working on amazing technology. Each of them contributes something to the movement in their own way and to their abilities and knowledge. This is what makes Free Software great and what brought the authors of this book together. This book is the answer to "What would you have liked to know when you started contributing". We are here to give you an insight into the many different areas. We are here to give you a head-start if you are new. We are here to give you some insight into other areas and projects if you have been contributing for a while already.
Join us for a fun ride through many different projects and areas of contribution.
